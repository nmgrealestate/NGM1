About NMG Estates

Founded in 2025, Nandd Mahal Group brings together over 25 years of real-estate expertisein Indore under one umbrella. We’ve built, sold and handed over landmark townships—always honoring our commitments and evolving through every challenge. Today, Nandd Mahal Group drives forward a diverse pipeline of projects, united by our core values of integrity, accountability and innovation.

  • Legacy of Delivery: From our inaugural Swasth Nagar colony in 1999 to today’s Khajrana residences, we’ve consistently completed and handed over communities on time.
  • Investor-First Mindset: We work closely with partners—like ISH and other seasoned developers—to structure transparent agreements and deliver on every promise.
  • Modern Living: Our upcoming and ongoing projects feature contemporary design, smart-home features and thoughtfully landscaped spaces

Vission

To be the foremost real-estate pioneer in Central India—transforming skylines, uplifting communities and setting new benchmarks in sustainable, smart living.

Misson

Nandd Mahal Group develops and delivers thoughtfully designed residential and mixed-use communities that blend modern amenities, transparent governance and investor-centric partnerships—while consistently meeting timelines and exceeding expectations.
